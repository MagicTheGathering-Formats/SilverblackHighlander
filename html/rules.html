Rules

Silverblack Highlander is a "Magic: The Gathering" singleton 1v1 format, allowing only commons and uncommons.

  • Maindeck of at least 50 cards
  • Sideboard of up to 5 cards
  • Silverblack: The deck must exclusively contain commons and uncommons. (If a card is listed as a common or uncommon on Gatherer in any set, it is legal.)
  • Singelton: Decks are restricted to a single instance of each card, except for basic lands and cards explicitly allowing multiple copies (e.g., Shadowborn Apostle).
  • Additionally, decks are constrained to a maximum of 5 points from the points list, which you can find here.
  • Players start the game with 20 life and play 1v1 matches.


In addition, the following cards are banned from Silverblack Highlander:

  • Ante cards
  • Conspiracy cards
  • Cards depicting racism and cultural insensitivity
  • Dexterity cards
  • Acorn cards
  • Silver-bordered cards
